UFO Sightings in America — Where, When, and What We See

This visualisation turns thousands of NUFORC reports into a guided tour of the American UFO imagination. We start wide—state and city maps show where reports cluster—then move through time to watch waves of sightings rise and fade. Finally, we zoom into the shapes people claim to see and when in the day they look up. The goal isn’t to prove or debunk anything; it’s to reveal patterns you can verify from the data—mixing geography, culture, and time into one story.
